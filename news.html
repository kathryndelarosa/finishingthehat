Current projects

At Actors Theatre, I split my time between the mainstage and the PTC seasons. I’m assisting with dramaturgy for The Wolves, as well as Jeff Augustin’s Where the Mountain Meets the Sea, Candrice Jones’ FLEX, and the PTC commission Are You There? by Vivian Barnes, Jonathan Norton and Gab Reisman in the Humana Festival. I served as dramaturg for half of the Solo Mio series written and performed by acting apprentices, which played in three rounds in September and October. I’m also at the helm of the PTC New Play Project commission Louisville // Kentucky // USA by Lisa Sanaye Dring.

In December, I found a second to workshop my play Ribs with University Players, culminating in a weekend of public readings at Indiana University.

After graduating this May, I was a literary assistant at The New Harmony Project, where I worked on Donnetta Lavinia Grays’ Where We Stand and lent dramaturgical support to Jennifer Barclay and Robert Schenkkan. I got my first professional dramaturgy credit under my belt with IU Summer Theatre’s Little Women.
