Kathryn de la Rosa

Work About News Plays

April

Vinegar Tom, Kathryn's final undergraduate dramaturgy credit, ended its run at IU Theatre.

Kathryn played Mary Bennet in a staged reading of Kaela Mei-Shing Garvin's TYA Pride and Prejudice adaptation.

May

Kathryn's play Ribs received a staged reading directed by Eleanor Sobczyk at IU Theatre.

Kathryn played God in a staged reading of the Big Ten Theatre Consortium commission Bonnets (How Ladies of Good Breeding are Induced to Murder) by Jen Silverman, directed by Madison Colquette for Granfalloon.

Kathryn assists in workshops of Donnetta Lavinia Grays' Where We Stand at the 2019 New Harmony Project Conference directed by Tamilla Woodard. She also lends support to writers-in-residence Jennifer Barclay and Robert Schenkkan.

June

Kathryn will serve as production dramaturg for IU Summer Theatre's production of Little Women, adapted by Marisha Chamberlain and directed by Jenny McKnight.

July

Kathryn returns to her old Kentucky home to join the Actors Theatre of Louisville 2019-2020 Professional Training Company as a dramaturgy & literary management apprentice.
