Kathryn de la Rosa is a Kentuckian dramaturg, writer, and an apprentice in the 2019-2020 Professional Training Company at Actors Theatre of Louisville. She holds a BA in theatre & drama and a BAJ in journalism from Indiana University Bloomington.

Previously, she interned in the literary office at Asolo Repertory Theatre and as a literary assistant at the 2019 New Harmony Project Conference. Her plays have been developed in IUâ€™s At First Sight new play festival and by University Players.

She has additional credits as a sound designer, performer and stage manager in productions and readings at IU Theatre, University Players and Jewish Theatre of Bloomington.

Kathryn is a 2013 creative writing alumna of the Kentucky Center Governor's School for the Arts. In her life as an audio producer, she worked on American Student Radio and The Quietus Hour, and contributed to programs at WFHB. She also designs for print and coded this website with her bare hands.

Her other interests include tarot, astrology and the Bon Appetit Test Kitchen.
