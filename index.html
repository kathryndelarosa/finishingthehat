© 2020 Kathryn de la Rosa

Kathryn de la Rosa is a queer Pilipina playwright, dramaturg and former college newspaper astrologer born and raised in Kentucky. Kathryn writes plays about identity and place, resistance and healing, spirituality and magic — the contradictions and conjunctions that disrupt power, binaries and division. With bachelor’s degrees in theater and journalism, Kathryn is a media jack-of-all-trades eternally interested in cross-platform, public-facing dramaturgy, and how technology shapes modern access and production. They live and create in Louisville.

They are available for writing, dramaturgy, audio, and digital freelance projects and collaborations. Ask about work samples.
