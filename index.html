Kathryn de la Rosa is a double Pisces and a dramaturg, sound designer and writer. She is a senior in her third and final season with IU Theatre. She will complete bachelor's degrees in theater and journalism in May 2019 with a thesis on ethics and audience trust in docudrama through Mike Daisey and This American Life.

Outside IU Theatre, Kathryn has designed and performed with University Players, read scripts for Bloomington Playwrights Project, and will design for Jewish Theatre of Bloomington after graduation. She spent the summer of 2018 interning in the literary office at Asolo Repertory Theatre in Sarasota, Florida.

Kathryn is a 2013 creative writing alumna of the Kentucky Center Governor's School for the Arts. She writes a weekly astrology column for the Indiana Daily Student. In her life as an audio producer, she worked on American Student Radio and The Quietus Hour, and contributed to programs at WFHB. She also designs for print and coded this website.
