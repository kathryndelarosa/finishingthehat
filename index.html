Hi! I’m Kathryn de la Rosa, a Kentuckian playwright and dramaturg. I was an apprentice at Actors Theatre of Louisville. Before that, I was the inaugural Indiana Daily Student astrologer.

I write plays set in Kentucky, Indiana and the Philippines, with love and complication.

I’m drawn to work that engages a diversity of place, race, and ideas, toward liberation from capital, colonialism and the state.

I see regional theater as the place to claim queer, rural and immigrant histories—and locate our futures.
