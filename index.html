Kathryn de la Rosa is a queer Pilipina playwright, dramaturg and former college newspaper astrologer born and raised in Kentucky. Kathryn writes plays about identity and place, resistance and healing, spirituality and magic — the contradictions and conjunctions that disrupt power, binaries and division. With bachelor’s degrees in theatre & drama and audio journalism, Kathryn is a media jack-of-all-trades eternally interested in cross-platform, public-facing dramaturgy.
