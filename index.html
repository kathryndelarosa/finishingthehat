Oh hey! It's me, Kathryn de la Rosa.

I’m a genderqueer Pilipina dramaturg & playwright born and bred in Kentucky, currently an apprentice at Actors Theatre of Louisville.

I like working on new plays, adaptations, and verbatim and verse drama. I write plays about identity: resistance and healing, spirituality and magic.

I’m a true jack-of-all-trades (emphasis on master of none): I coded with website with my bare hands, lived past lives as radio producer and sound designer, and seriously study and practice natal astrology.

I graduated from Indiana University with a BA in theater and journalism. I’m available for writing, production dramaturgy, script consultation, and audio & digital projects.
