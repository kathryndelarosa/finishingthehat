mabuhay

Kathryn de la Rosa (they/she) is a Filipinx writer, dramaturg & audio producer from Kentucky.

They work where provincialism and diaspora meet, toward liberation from capital, colonialism and the state. Plays include common wealth, or this is my diversity statement, Ribs, and Holy Virgins. Kathryn contributes to Queer Kentucky and writes a free newsletter called elder zoomer on subjects like TV, theater, Taylor Swift, folk religion, and the death of neoliberalism. She wrote an astrology column for the Indiana Daily Student and had poetry published in Canvas magazine.

Find them on New Play Exchange and POC in Audio.

Kathryn was a 2019–20 dramaturgy apprentice at Actors Theatre of Louisville, and previously interned with The New Harmony Project.

BA + BAJ Indiana University.
