I am a Louisville-based playwright, dramaturg and freelance audio producer & digital creator.

I write plays set in Kentucky, Indiana and the Philippines, with love and complication. I’m drawn to work that engages a diversity of place, race and ideas, toward liberation from liberation from capital, colonialism, and the state.

I also produce podcasts and code websites from scratch.
