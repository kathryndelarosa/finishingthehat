Kathryn de la Rosa (b. 1997, Prestonsburg, Ky.) is a Pisces and a dramaturg, writer and multimedia producer currently in the the Professional Training Company at Actors Theatre of Louisville.

Kathryn earned a B.A. in theatre & drama and journalism from Indiana University Bloomington, where she worked on mainstage and student productions as a dramaturg, technician, and performer. Her plays have been developed in IU’s At First Sight new play festival and by University Players. She graduated cum laude with a thesis on Mike Daisey’s appearance on This American Life.

Previously, she served as a literary assistant in the 2019 New Harmony Project Conference, and interned in the literary office at Asolo Repertory Theatre. She is a 2013 creative writing alumna of the Kentucky Center Governor’s School for the Arts, where she returned on summer staff in 2016.
