I am a Louisville-based playwright, dramaturg, and freelance audio & digital producer.

I write plays set in Kentucky, Indiana and the Philippines, with love and complication.

I’m drawn to work that engages a diversity of place, race and ideas, toward liberation from liberation from capital, colonialism, and the state.

Available for freelance projects and collaborations in: production dramaturgy, script reading & consultation, audio production, copywriting & editing, front-end web design, and social curation.
