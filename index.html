Kathryn de la Rosa [she/her]
Dramaturg using media for modern production and engagement.

I’m a Kentuckian dramaturg, writer, sound designer, and multimedia producer. I believe theater can and should serve America across social and geographic difference.

I will complete bachelor’s degrees in theater and journalism in 2019 with a thesis on audience trust in docudrama. My media background taught me to priortize clarity and presentation, which informs my dramaturgical practice in and after rehearsal. I have additional experience in radio and web design, including this website.

While I love research, I've spent undergrad intervening a flawed canon. I hope to develop new plays by rural playwrights and playwrights of color.

I also write a weekly astrology column for the Indiana Daily Student.

Drop a line if you want to work together, whether it's a script in progress, funky new art, or even a tarot reading.
