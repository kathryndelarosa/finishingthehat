Freelance Dramaturgy

Kathryn is an alum of apprentice & intern companies at Actors Theatre of Louisville & The New Harmony Project passionate about new plays. Thanks to their history-heavy undergrad, Kathryn is also an experienced, curious & dialogical dramaturg of the Western canon, with a big nerdy interest in dialectical theater, verbatim drama and Theatre of the Oppressed. If you vibe with tarot & analogies from the Bible to Brecht to The Bachelor, get in touch!
