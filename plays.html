Ribs

4f, full-length — “Did you know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and privilege from the strange, funny, vulgar confines of a Bloomington student rental. Between The Bachelor and graduation, Title IX and astrology, Margo, Liha, Josie, and Katie learn to feel and fight at the end of a decade in middle America.

this is my diversity statement

7f + 3m, full-length — When a racist incident at an arts summer camp forces eight college-aged counselors to do damage control, what's right, true, and wrong? What is a safe space? Who constructs them, and at whose cost? On liberalism, fragility, progress, and TikTok teens.

Holy Virgins

3f + 1nb, one-act — Kentucky, 2012: Sam, Marina and Lenny bail on Confirmation class after a Satanic prank gone wrong.

Pasyon Play

3f + 8m, one-act — In the Philippines, 1970, a man asks to be crucified, and a family at the heart of staging Christ's passion and death grapples with transformation: changing players, postcolonial politics, and performance as penance. After Sarah Ruhl.

first love / late spring

2 flexible, one-act — They meet in autumn, fall in winter, and leave in spring. A selfish millennial pandemic non-breakup play.
