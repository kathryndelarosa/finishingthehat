Kathryn de la Rosa

Work About News Plays

Ribs

4w, full-length

“Did you guys know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. Four girls grow up in their last four months of undergrad: Margo, an ex-College Republican; would-be witch Katie; Josie, hardened and wry; and meme-illiterate Liha. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, specific to women who go to IU Bloomington. The Bachelor's Colton Underwood, Discalced Carmelites, and the natyashastra coalesce into a messy, braless exploration of female sexuality.

University Players workshop, fall 2019; May 2019 staged reading and 2019 At First Sight Festival of New Plays excerpted reading dir. Eleanor Sobczyk.

Skin

1w, monologue

On chronic conditions, like eczema, bad mothers and colonialism. Isda is a brash and furious Pinay careening toward a mystical horizon, a mental breakdown, or both in the middle of America. A solo show for a Filipina performer.
