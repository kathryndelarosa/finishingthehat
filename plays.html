Ribs

“Did you know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and privilege from the strange, funny, vulgar confines of a Bloomington student rental. Between The Bachelor and graduation, Title IX and astrology, Margo, Liha, Josie, and Katie learn to feel — and fight — in the middle of America.

Crocodile Play (for Lolong)

An imagining of the death and lives of the once-largest crocodile in activity. Crocodile hunter Lolong dies days before his team captures and names a 20-foot crocodile in his honor, now kept as a tourist attraction at the Bunawan Ecopark and Wildlife Conservation Center. When his Filipina-American granddaugther Aimee returns for his funeral, she and her aunt untangle the political, historical and supernatural effects of Lolong’s capture as the line between dead man and dying reptile blurs.
