Available on New Play Exchange. My plays have been developed at Indiana University in the At First Sight New Play Festival and by University Players, and independently with some of my 19/20 Actors Theatre apprentice cohort.

this is my diversity statement — 7f + 3m, full-length

When a racist incident at an arts summer camp forces eight college-aged counselors to do damage control, what's right? What's true? Who constructs safe space, and at whose cost? On liberalism, the non-profit sector, and TikTok teens.

Ribs — 4f, full-length

"Did you know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and privilege from the strange, funny, vulgar confines of a Bloomington student rental — and the highs and horrors of being a woman at a state university.

Pasyon Play — 3f + 8m, one-act

In the Philippines, 1970, a man asks to be crucified. A family at the heart of staging Christ's death grapples with transformation: changing players, postcolonial politics, and penance. After Sarah Ruhl.

Holy Virgins — 3f + 1nb, one-act

Sam, Marina and Lenny bail on Confirmation class after a Satanic prank gone awry.

first love / late spring — 2, one-act

They meet in autumn, fall in winter, and leave in spring. A selfish millennial pandemic non-breakup play.
