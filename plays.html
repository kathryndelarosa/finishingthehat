about

I live and create at the intersection of diaspora and provinciality. My plays are set in Kentucky, Indiana and the Philippines, with love and complication. I’m drawn to work that engages a diversity of place, race, and ideas. I believe in regional theater, the oversoul, and astrology. I’m a digital jack-of-all-trades ready to forge new ways of access and connection to live theater.
